# Founder Ownership & Equity Declaration
Version 1.0 — © Appatize Ltd.

This document asserts ownership and equity rights relating to Appatize.

## Founder Ownership
The intellectual property, architecture, concepts, and inventions 
within Appatize were created by the Founder.  
All rights are retained exclusively by Appatize Ltd.

## Equity Structure
- Founder holds 100% equity unless otherwise amended.  
- No contributor, contractor, or collaborator automatically earns equity.  
- Equity grants require a written, signed agreement.

## Control
Founder retains full creative, strategic, operational, and technical control.

## IP Continuity
All future work, expansions, and derivative systems remain the property of Appatize Ltd.
